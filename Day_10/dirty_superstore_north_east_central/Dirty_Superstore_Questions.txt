Part A: Loading & Structural Data Inspection (Questions Only)

- superstore_north_dirty.csv
- superstore_east_dirty.csv
- superstore_central_dirty.csv

--------------------------------------------------
Section 1: Dataset Loading
--------------------------------------------------

Q1.
Load the three regional Superstore datasets into three separate dataframes.
Display the first five records from each dataset and report their dimensions
(number of rows and columns).

Q2.

three datasets.

Section 2: Structural Schema Inspection
--------------------------------------------------

Q3.

Q4.

categories:
- Date/Time attributes
- Numeric attributes
- Categorical/Text attributes

Section 3: Cross-Dataset Structural Comparison
--------------------------------------------------

Q5.

- Whether column names are identical

State whether the datasets are structurally compatible for direct combination.

Q6.
Identify any columns that appear to be:
- Index-related or auto-generated
- Metadata or notes fields not relevant for analysis

Justify your answer using structural evidence.
